Domestic Cat

The oldest known pet cat existed 9,500 years ago - in 2004, it's grave was discovered in Cyprus by French archaeologists.

  • Scientific Name: Felis catus
  • Average Length: 76.2 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 14 years
  • Habitat: Human dwellings

As of 2017, the domestic cat was the second-most popular pet in the United States by number of pets owned. It has been suggested that cats were first domesticated in the Middle East to help with controlling pests around human settlements, namely house mice!
